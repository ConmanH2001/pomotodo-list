Pomodoro Timer and Todo list!

A Pomodoro timer is a type of timer that gives you a 25 minute work time and a 5 minute break. After abput four of those breaks you usually get a 15 minute break, then after 4 of those is a 30 minute break. This will be a timer but it will also have a todo list that shows what you have to do, what you have completed, and the percentage of what has been completed.
